The Genre Of The Year

As a part of a Digital Humanities course for computer science students from Ben Gurion University, we analyzed people's favorite genres from 1941 to 2016. The process include parsing of "Top 100 Billboards" tables, extracting the data from the internet, sorting into genres and years, and finally visualizing the results.
